Cyber Security: The importance of the defence of computer
and network

Cybersecurity is a procedure that defends computers, networks, servers, electronic systems,
mobile devices, and data from attacks that can be spiteful. Cybersecurity is also known to keep
security of electronic information and security of information technology.

It also prevents attacks that intend to interrupt the operations of system’s or device’s.

The importance of cybersecurity keeps growing as there are more users, programs and devices
increasing in the modern enterprise.

There are different sections in cybersecurity like network security, cloud security,
application security, critical infrastructure security, etc. The future of cybersecurity is very bright due to
the number of threats and attackers increasing as we get introduced to new technology now and then.
