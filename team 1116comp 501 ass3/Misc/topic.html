Chosen Technology/Topic

The page or area describing your chosen technology/topic and why it is significant and/or has important implications for society.

There are many types of threats in cybersecurity. It is very tough to keep up with latest technologies and security
trends however it is extremely important to protect information from cyber threats.

There is a form of software called malware that can be used to create risks for computer users, the risks can include
viruses, worms, etc.

Another type of malware is known as ransomware. In ransomware, an attacker locks the computer system files of
the victim mostly by encryption and then ask for payment in order to decrypt and unlock the computer system files.

Another threat is known as phishing in which fraudulent messages or emails that look very similar to the ones from
known sources are sent. These attacks are random most of the times. The purpose of these messages are to steal
the data that is sensitive, for example login data and credit card information.
